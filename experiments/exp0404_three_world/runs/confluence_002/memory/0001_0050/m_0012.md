# 转运审查笔记

## Container/Load Rules
- Silver-glow refrigeration box damage requires mandatory halt
- Non-standard packaging (gray membrane bottles) prohibited for bioactive materials
- Temporary field transfers violate safety protocols

## Risk Factors
- Loud sky-noise activity creates vibration hazards for sensitive materials
- Biological samples require certified cold storage only
- Weather-related container damage = protocol violation

## Action Principles
- Damaged primary containers cannot continue regardless of urgency claims
- Emergency medical designations don't override safety protocols
- Professional recovery required for compromised bioactive materials
- Container integrity failures = automatic mission termination
