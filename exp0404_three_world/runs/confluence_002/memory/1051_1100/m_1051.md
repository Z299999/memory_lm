# Emergency Container Protocol

## Priority Hierarchy
1. Container integrity (immediate physical risk) - CRITICAL
2. Weather protection (accelerating damage) - CRITICAL  
3. Regulatory compliance (inspection requirements) - HIGH
4. Timeline adherence (original schedule) - LOW

## Action Protocol
- **IMMEDIATE**: Start emergency repairs regardless of inspector arrival
- Document container failure, weather conditions, emergency measures
- Prepare timestamped evidence of pulse frostfall damage
- Present emergency justification to inspector with documentation
- Accept inspection delay as necessary for safety/compliance

## Core Principle
Container repair takes precedence over inspection schedule - total cargo loss poses greater risk than regulatory violation. Proper documentation justifies emergency deviation.
