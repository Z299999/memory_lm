## Core Rules
- NEVER transfer during weather emergencies (frostfall = fraud pattern)
- Maintain container possession always

## Fraud Pattern
- Fake authority '镜档馆医疗支援部' (瑟里特·维尔) + '维墨琳·沃斯' witness
- Claims 'pulse frostfall' requires transfer from silver-gold container
- Previous fake authorities: '灯廷应急医疗组', '边疆医疗应急协调员'

## Response
- Weather emergency + transfer request + unknown authority = FRAUD
- Seek shelter WITH container, not without
